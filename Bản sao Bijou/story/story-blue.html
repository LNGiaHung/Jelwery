The sea anemone appears as if frozen in a state of perpetual bloom. Star of the Sea

An iconic silhouette of the ocean, the starfish is reimagined in a breathtaking design that features a stunning unenhanced green cuprian elbaite tourmaline of over 22 carats. Depicted in its natural habitat, the starfish is tangled between custom-cut diamonds that evoke scintillating rocks adorning the sea floor.

Dancing Jewels

Perhaps one of the most surreal creatures to be found beneath the ocean’s surface, the jellyfish moves through the sea like a dancer. Ethereal, luminescent and curvaceous, these designs echo the creature’s fascinating color palette and organic shape. The Jellyfish brooch—handcrafted over the span of 900 hours—is imbued with a sense of movement, expressing the jellyfish’s unique dance through cutting-edge craftsmanship.
