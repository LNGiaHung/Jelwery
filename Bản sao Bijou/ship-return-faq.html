What products are eligible for returns?

Products are accepted for credit or exchange in the U.S. within 30 days of purchase. All merchandise must be in perfect saleable condition, with the sales receipt. Exclusions apply, please note:

  • Merchandise under 100 million will be accepted for return or exchange within 30 days of purchase.
  • Merchandise 100 million or more will be eligible for exchange within 30 days of purchase. No refunds will be issued for purchases above 100 million.
  • Merchandise must be accompanied by all original packaging for a refund. Including jewelry boxes and pouches.
  • Certain limited edition and collaboration merchandise may be ineligible for a refund.
